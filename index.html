FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age criteria for Pre-KG and LKG?

Pre-KG is for children aged 2.5 to 3.5 years, and LKG is for children aged 3.5 to 5 years as of June 1st of the academic year.

What are the school timings?

School operates from 9:00 AM to 12:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Optional daycare extension is available until 4:00 PM.

Is transport facility available?

Yes, we provide safe van transport covering nearby areas. Please contact us for route availability and fees.

What documents are needed for admission?

Birth certificate, Aadhaar card, passport-size photos, and previous school records (if applicable) are required during registration.

How do I track my child's progress?

We share regular photo updates, monthly progress reports, and conduct parent-teacher meetings every term.
